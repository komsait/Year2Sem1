Need

A huge amount of food is wasted at the end of the working day in many restaurants. There is a lack of efficient distribution channels for surplus food from restaurants to those in need. Additionally, there is an increasing demand for support and resources among individuals and families facing food insecurity. Restaurant owners are seeking efficient ways to manage surplus food. As Muslims, the most important thing is that saving food is a way of thanking Allah for His blessing.

Approach

1. Direct Engagement

Restaurants can easily sign up and schedule surplus food pickups. We collect this food and deliver it to local charities. This streamlined process ensures that good food doesn't go to waste.

2. Online Donation System

The process of how restaurants can input the wasted food into a cart on the website for donation.

3. Food Collection Process

Our organization will visit the restaurants to collect the donated food and ensure it reaches the charity organizations or those in need.

4. Distribution Responsibility

The charity organizations or donors will be responsible for distributing the collected food to those who need it.

Benefits

1. Environmental Impact

Reduced Food Waste: Our platform helps significantly reduce the amount of food waste from restaurants.

Lower Carbon Footprint: By donating surplus food, restaurants contribute to a reduction in methane emissions from landfills.

2. Community Support

Feeding the Needy: Surplus food is redirected to local charities, helping to feed the hungry and support community welfare.

Strengthening Community Ties: Participation enhances the restaurant's role as a socially responsible entity within the community.

3. Economic Benefits

Cost Savings: Restaurants save on waste disposal costs.

Competition/Alternatives

1. Composting

Composting: Reduces waste but doesn't feed people.

Food Save: Reduces waste and feeds those in need with no extra effort.

2. Direct Donations

Direct Donations: Require logistics and tracking efforts from the restaurant.

Food Save: Streamlines pickups and provides easy tracking.

3. Other Food Donation Platforms

Other Platforms: Complex interfaces and limited integration.

Food Save: Simple, user-friendly platform with comprehensive features.

4. Waste Disposal Services

Waste Disposal: Costs money and lacks social impact.

Food Save: Offers potential cost savings and supports the community.
